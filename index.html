About me

Front End Developer

Hi there! I'm Gundala Mahesh Babu,my journey to web development might seem unconventional. With a background in Electronics and Communication Engineering, I've always been fascinated by the underlying technology that powers our digital world. This curiosity naturally led me to explore web development, where I discovered a passion for building user-friendly and visually stunning websites.

My engineering background gives me a solid foundation in problem-solving and understanding the technical aspects of web applications. While I'm proficient in front-end development utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, my knowledge extends to the back-end as well. I'm comfortable working with languages like Java and database systems like MySQL, allowing me to create robust and well-structured web experiences.

I'm eager to apply my skills and knowledge in a practical setting. I'm a fast learner and believe I can contribute to the team while gaining valuable experience under your mentorship.
